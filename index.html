Reason why we love polar bears

Exceptional Swimmers: Polar bears are incredible swimmers and can cover distances of over 60 miles (97 kilometers) in a single swim. They have been known to swim continuously for up to 10 hours at a time, thanks to their large, webbed paws that act like paddles.
Black Skin Under White Fur: Despite their white fur, polar bears actually have black skin underneath. The black skin helps them absorb and retain heat from the sun.
Powerful Predators: Polar bears are at the top of the Arctic food chain and primarily feed on seals. They have a highly developed sense of smell, allowing them to detect seals nearly a mile away and through several feet of compacted snow and ice.
Large Body Size: Male polar bears can weigh between 900 to 1,600 pounds (400 to 720 kilograms), making them the largest land carnivores. Female polar bears are smaller, typically weighing between 330 to 550 pounds (150 to 250 kilograms).
